Through it all - January 28, 2007

Well, here I am. After considerable health issues and an ice storm that cripple our city (we had no power for 12 days) God has proven Himself faithful. Did we ever doubt it?

I'm looking forward to getting back to vocalizing, rehearsals and getting back into the mainstream of things. I have certainly missed doing what I was created to do.

Thank you for all your prayers. As you know the cancer is gone, the surgery for a deviated septum on Jan. 5th went well and I'm breathing a whole lot better. The therapy for my vocal chords has been going well and I look forward to getting back on the road. I've missed you all and can't wait to see you in person.

I'm also looking forward to getting back in the studio and recording another CD.
For 2007 one of our goals is to get our WEB-TV programming up and going. We hope to do a news show, Bible Study show, and a music video/interview show with local Christian bands. If you are not on our mailing list to receive our newsletter, please send me an e-mail with your postal address. God Bless-

YAHOO! - December 4, 2006

Boy am I glad there are praying people out there! I went in for a routine physical in Sept. The doctor found a mole that looked suspicious and sent me to have it biopsied. So on my wedding anniversary (Nov. 10th) I went to the dermatologist for a biopsy. While at the dermatologist I requested he look at a growth on my arm.

The dermatologist thought that the mole was malanoma and the growth basel cell carcinoma. He biopsied both sites.
The results? The mole benign. The growth on my arm, pre-malanoma. We scheduled surgery.A procedure called MOHS was performed (I got to watch the whole thing and it was cool) Four stitches on the inside and eleven on the outside later, I am happy to report that the pathology report came back this morning, clean. They got it all. Thank GOD! I get my stitches out on Friday.
Thank you for all who lift me up in prayer! Keep Praying - God listens AND answers!

Nicaragua - September 27, 2006

Wow! What a trip! I'm recovering from the trip to Nicaragua. It was awesome! Over 50 salvations, 10 miracle healings, Praise The Lord! It made daily, random water and electrical shortages all worthwhile. The concert for the youth was an overwhelming success. The attendee ages were from 8 years old to 32 (with a few older folks in the mix).

As I travel throughout the world, I find that all youth have the same desires, concerns, problems and needs. It was an incredible honor to be used by God to minister to those needs, to counsel one on one and to fellowship with the Nicaraguan people.

I look forward to returning to Nicaragua soon.
