Graham J. Barclay - Soundwave FM (Jun 7, 2007)
Now that's how to start a gospel CD! because you can't go wrong with the Dottie Rambo classic 'I Go To The Rock', a song genetically programmed to get even the tardiest foot tapping.

And it's the up tempo numbers that not only grab you the hardest but which give Ms St. Johns voice a chance to shine. there's a gritty edge to the likes of 'Operator' that is missing from the jazzier, mellower numbers like 'That Voice'.

I'd give a shout out to the backing singers but due to a paucity of credits I can't. Same goes for the melodic guitar lines on 'The Blood Will Never Lose Its Power'. With some judicious use of the programme button you'll end up with a delightful gospel / praise CD. Just make sure you leave the acapella 'His Eye Is On The Sparrow' till the end, for maximum impact.
